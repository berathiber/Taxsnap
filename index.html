🍁 Input Tax Credits (ITCs)

If you're a GST/HST registrant, you can recover tax paid on business expenses:

  • HST Provinces (ON, NB, NS, NL, PE): Full HST claimable
  • GST-only (AB, NT, NU, YT): 5% GST claimable
  • GST + PST (BC, SK, MB): Only GST claimable; PST is not recoverable
  • Quebec: GST as ITC; QST claimed separately on QC return

⚠️ Keep original receipts. CRA requires them for ITCs over $30.

🍁 Canadian Tax Guide

    

      

        HST (Harmonized Sales Tax)
        
Combined tax in ON (13%), NB, NS, NL, PE (15%). Full HST claimable as ITC.

      

      

        GST — 5% Federal
        
Applies everywhere. Always claimable as ITC for registered businesses.

      

      

        PST (Provincial)
        
BC 7%, SK 6%, MB 7%. PST is not recoverable on federal return.

      

      

        QST — 9.975% (Quebec)
        
Claimed as ITR on Quebec return, separate from federal GST ITC.
