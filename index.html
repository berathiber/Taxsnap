🍁 Canadian Tax Guide

  
HST (Harmonized Sales Tax)
Combined tax in ON (13%), NB, NS, NL, PE (15%). Full HST claimable as ITC.

  
GST — 5% Federal
Applies everywhere. Always claimable as ITC for registered businesses.

  
PST (Provincial)
BC 7%, SK 6%, MB 7%. PST is not recoverable on federal return.

  
QST — 9.975% (Quebec)
Claimed as ITR on Quebec return, separate from federal GST ITC.

  
📌 Filing Tips

  
  • Keep originals for all business expenses
  • CRA requires receipts for ITCs over $30
  • Meals: only 50% claimable
  • Use CSV export to simplify filing
